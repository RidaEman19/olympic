How we work together:

We pair program weekly, review content with athletes and coaches, and ship small improvements continuously. Every feature maps to a clear user need—results, schedules, and stories—backed by ethical design and inclusive language.

  • Performance: 95+ Lighthouse scores on mobile for key pages.
  • Accessibility: WCAG 2.2 AA coverage with semantic markup and keyboard-first navigation.
  • Scale: Component library serving schedules, medal tables, and profiles across events.
  • Content: Athlete stories localized for 8 regions with consistent editorial voice.
