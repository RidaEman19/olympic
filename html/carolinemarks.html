CAROLINE MARKS

biography

Despite being just 19 years old at Tokyo 2020, Caroline Marks was already comfortably in the top six places in the World Surfing League rankings - just a few spots below her idol, Carissa Moore. Born the third of six children, sport has been the centre of Marks' life. According to WSL, their back yard in Florida had a moto track, halfpipe and a surf break across the street that helped ignite her love of the sport. Marks carved her path on the waves of Melbourne Beach, where she honed the skills that would carry her to making history.
