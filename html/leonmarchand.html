LEON MARCHAND

biography

Leon Marchand has emerged as a rising star of French swimming, boasting an impressive collection of World Championships, NCAA titles, and European crowns. Marchand's profile rose significantly before the Paris 2024 Olympic Games. To add to the intrigue, his coach is none other than Michael Phelps' former trainer, who is considered to be one of the greatest swimming mentors in the world. Considered a promising swimmer during his first Olympic Games in Tokyo, Marchand became an icon of French sport at the Olympic Games Paris 2024 Olympic Games. The swimmer entered the history books of swimming by winning four individual gold medals in Paris, including two on the same day in the 200m breaststroke and 200m butterfly. Along with his four titles at Paris 2024, Marchand achieved four Olympic records, with his remarkable feats captivating the world. In recognition of his remarkable achievements, he carried the Olympic flame during the closing ceremony, ending his second Olympic Games in spectacular fashion.
