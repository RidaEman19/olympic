Simone Biles

Artistic Gymnast • United States • Multiple-time World and Olympic medalist

Biography

Simone Biles is widely regarded as one of the greatest gymnasts of all time. Known for her extraordinary difficulty and execution, she has set numerous records and inspired a new generation through resilience and excellence on the world stage.

Major achievements

  • Olympic Gold: Team, All-Around, Vault, Floor (Rio 2016)
  • World Championships: Most world medals and golds by a gymnast
  • Eponymous Skills: Multiple elements named “Biles” across vault and floor
  • Laureus Awards: Multiple Sportswoman of the Year honors

Fun facts

  • Signature: The “Biles” on floor involves a double layout with a half twist.
  • Advocacy: Vocal supporter of athlete mental health and safety.
